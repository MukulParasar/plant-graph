SCOPE:
Annual internal inspection of Boiler Drum B-201 per OISD-STD-118 and PESO
statutory requirements for pressure vessels operating above 40 bar. Visual
and UT thickness survey of drum shell, nozzle welds, and internals.

FINDINGS:
Shell thickness within acceptable range (nominal 22mm, measured 21.4mm).
Isolation Valve V-205 seat showed minor erosion, recommended for seat
lapping at next shutdown. Safety Relief Valve RV-210 tested and popped at
61.4 bar (within +/-2% of 62 bar set point) - PASS. Level Transmitter
LT-108 calibration verified against reference gauge - PASS.

No indications of shell cracking or corrosion beyond acceptable limits.
Internal cyclone separators intact. Feedwater inlet nozzle (connected to
Economiser Inlet Header EH-201) shows no erosion.
