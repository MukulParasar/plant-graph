DOCUMENT TYPE: Standard Operating Procedure
DOCUMENT ID: SOP-BFW-04
TITLE: Boiler Feed Pump Changeover (Duty/Standby Switch)
UNIT: Unit 12 - Boiler Feed Water System
REVISION: Rev B
EFFECTIVE DATE: 01-Jan-2024
OWNER: Operations Department, Custodian - Suresh Rao, Plant Manager

PURPOSE:
To provide a safe sequence for switching duty from Boiler Feed Pump
P-101A to standby Boiler Feed Pump P-101B (or vice versa) without
disturbing Feed Water Control Valve FCV-102 setpoint or tripping
Level Transmitter LT-108 interlock on Boiler Drum B-201.

PROCEDURE STEPS:
1. Confirm FIC-103 flow controller in MANUAL mode before changeover.
2. Start standby pump (e.g. P-101B) and confirm discharge pressure
   on PT-101 matches running pump within 2 bar before proceeding.
3. Gradually open standby pump discharge valve while throttling
   outgoing pump, monitoring TT-104 for thermal shock.
4. Confirm LT-108 remains above Low-Low trip point (15%) throughout.
5. Stop outgoing pump, isolate per lockout-tagout requirements of
   OISD-STD-118, tag out at Switchgear Panel SG-04.
6. Log changeover event with Shift Engineer and note any vibration
   abnormality for entry against Vibration Transmitter VT-101 trend.
7. Return FIC-103 to AUTO mode once flow stabilizes at 42 T/hr setpoint.

SAFETY NOTES:
Any deviation observed in RV-210 relief valve behavior or drum level
during changeover must be reported immediately and cross-referenced
against Inspection Report INS-2024-0056.
