EQUIPMENT: Boiler Feed Pump P-101A
UNIT: Unit 12 - Boiler Feed Water System
RAISED DATE: 03-Jun-2024
COMPLETION DATE: 07-Jun-2024
RAISED BY: Vikram Singh (Shift Engineer)
ASSIGNED TO: Mechanical Maintenance Team, Lead - Prakash Iyer

FAULT DESCRIPTION:
Vibration alarm triggered on P-101A (Vibration Transmitter VT-101,
reading 9.2 mm/s against alarm limit of 7.1 mm/s). Bearing housing
temperature elevated to 78C against normal 55C. Suspected drive-end
bearing degradation, consistent with lubrication contamination noted in
previous Inspection Report INS-2024-0032 (Feb-2024).

WORK PERFORMED:
Replaced drive-end and non-drive-end bearings on P-101A. Verified
alignment against Feed Water Control Valve FCV-102 piping loads. Checked
Motor MCC (Switchgear Panel SG-04) insulation resistance - within limits.
Reference Safety Procedure SOP-BFW-04 followed for pump changeover to
standby unit P-101B during works, per OISD-STD-118 lockout-tagout clause.

ROOT CAUSE (Preliminary):
Recurrent bearing failures on P-101A logged 3 times in past 18 months
(WO-2023-4410, WO-2023-6120, WO-2024-8871). Pattern suggests inadequate
lubrication interval rather than isolated defect - flagged for RCA review.

PARTS USED: SKF 6316 bearing set (2), grease cartridge type EP2.
DOWNTIME: 6.5 hours (within planned maintenance window).

Verified by: Anjali Deshmukh, Chief Engineer
