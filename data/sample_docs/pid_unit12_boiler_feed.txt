DESCRIPTION:
Boiler feed water is drawn from Deaerator Tank DA-101 and pumped by Boiler
Feed Pump P-101A (duty) and P-101B (standby) through Feed Water Control
Valve FCV-102 into Economiser Inlet Header EH-201. Pressure is monitored by
transmitter PT-101 and temperature by TT-104, both reporting to DCS Loop
LP-2201. Flow is regulated using Flow Indicating Controller FIC-103 with a
set point of 42 T/hr under normal load.

Downstream of the economiser, feed water enters Boiler Drum B-201 via
Isolation Valve V-205. A safety relief valve RV-210 (set at 62 bar) protects
the drum shell against overpressure per OISD-STD-118 requirements for
boiler pressure parts. Level is monitored by Level Transmitter LT-108
interlocked to trip Feed Pump P-101A on Low-Low level (LT-108 < 15%).

Motor MCC feed to P-101A and P-101B is rated 132kW, 415V, sourced from
Switchgear Panel SG-04. Equipment tag cross-reference: P-101A, P-101B,
FCV-102, PT-101, TT-104, FIC-103, EH-201, B-201, V-205, RV-210, LT-108.

Related documents: Maintenance Work Order WO-2024-8871 (P-101A bearing
replacement), Inspection Report INS-2024-0056 (Boiler Drum B-201 annual
inspection), Safety Procedure SOP-BFW-04 (Boiler Feed Pump Changeover).
